Have a Beer!

This site takes a look at some beer data from Beer Me! which has a list of almost 10,000 craft beers from 65 different countries.

A Quick Sample

The number of craft beer breweries has spiked over the last decade has spiked in the United States. According to Brewers Association, in 2008 there were just over 1,500 craft breweries, but by 2017 that number had reached over 6,000. However, Beer Revenue has grown at a much smaller rate, and average consumption per capita has slightly declined in the last several years according to Statista.

This project just wanted to focus on that market through the perspective of one beer connoisseur from Nebraska, Richard Stueven, who has traveled for over 25 years sampling approximately 10,000 beers from different breweries around the world. Take a look at the data and comparisons above.
